About Us

An NGO run by a group of passionate, energetic and like minded people towards achieving a common objective of eradicating garbage dumps and directing the residents towards achieving a cleaner and greener environment.

LBTC provides it's volunteers a platform filled with zest to contribute more to the society along with a sense of accomplishment and satisfaction.

Bengaluru Plog Run

Bengaluru Plog Run combines jogging with picking up dry plastic waste, in an effort to make Bengaluru a cleaner city.

Clay Ganapathi Workshop

As a tradition, idols of Lord Ganesha are hand made,worshiped and are immersed in rivers, lakes or wells. This event showcases the need for eco-friendly Ganesha idols.

Musical Cleanathon

TeamLBTC in association with Radio City and Onthara Bannagalu movie team are beautifying a 50 meters garbage dump near Banashankari Sub Register Office.
